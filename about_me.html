My story

To begin with, I must say that I'm just a regular girl who lives a normal life, but I like it. I'm 21 year old student from Croatia. I study multimedia and design. It's difficult to be a student. All that stress and obligations, especially when you're the kind of student that does everything at the last minute. Well, that's me. I found a way to turn my mind down and it's drawing. Since I started college, drawing became my number one hobby,

actually I didn't draw so often before. Well the more I think about it I don't have any other hobby. So I tough making a blog would be nice to spend my free time and if I earn a few coins here and there it would be great to invest it in better stuff for drawing. I would like to show you guys some of my works.
